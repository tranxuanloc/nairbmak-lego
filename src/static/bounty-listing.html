Candidate 1 - Creative uses of the Ohmni Arm 52321 KATT

Entrants will submit a proposal. Kambria Foundation will donate Ohmni arms to the top N projects.

Goal is for them to perform tasks with the Ohmni arm plus any additional 3d printed hardware/electronics that they build. These will all be open sourced on Kambria after the competition is over.

Examples:

  • Artist robot arm - create a nice compliant pen/marker mount and have the arm draw
  • Cleaning robot arm - attach a mister and spray and/or wipe surfaces using the arm
  • Screwdriver arm - be able to place screws into an assembly

Marks will be awarded for creativity and technical difficulty. Contestants
